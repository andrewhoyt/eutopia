Mastery of Progress: Cornerstones & Capstones

Time-Variable Progression

Advancement is untethered from age. Students move forward purely by demonstrating competency in PBL stacks aligned with Interest Cohorts.

Annual Cornerstone Projects

PreK–11 students complete a major project each year that serves as tangible proof of academic mastery for their Mastery Transcript.

The Senior Capstone

12th graders synthesize their journey through a final apprenticeship with partners like BNSF, Union Pacific, or Luminant.
